Cheese Balls

Description

In this simple and easy recipe you will make cheese balls. Ever since I tried these at a Brazilian Steak House I have always wanted to know how to make them myself. A couple years ago I found this recipe and have been consistantly making it for every holiday and friend gathering I have attended. I hope you enjoy this recipe as much as I do!

Ingredients

  • Tapioca Flour - 2 Cups
  • Eggs - 2 Large Eggs
  • Olive Oil - 1/2 Cups
  • Milk - 2/3 Cups
  • Shredded Mozzarella - 1/2 Cups
  • Parmigiano/Assorted Cheese - 2 Oz
  • Salt/Pepper

Steps

  1. Warm milk and oil to boiling in a pan.
  2. Add boiling milk and oil to flour. Mix and wait 5 minutes.
  3. Scramble eggs.
  4. Add cheese and eggs to flour. Mix.
  5. Form into spoon sized spheres on a baking mat.
  6. Bake 20 minutes at 400°
